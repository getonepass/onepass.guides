# How to Verify Your Company's Contact Email Address

Verifying your company's contact email on OnePass ensures that your organization can be reached through an authenticated and trusted email address. This enhances credibility and trust with investors and partners.

## Why Verify Your Contact Email?

- **Confirms authenticity** and ensures reliable communication.
- **Prevents fraud and impersonation**.
- **Displays a verification badge** on your company profile, increasing credibility.

## Verification Steps

Follow these steps to verify your company's contact email:

### Initiate Email Verification

1. Navigate to **OnePass Account Settings** > **Email Verification**.
2. Enter your official company email address (e.g., `contact@yourcompany.com`).
3. Click **Send Verification Email**.

### Confirm the Verification Email

1. Check the inbox of the email address you provided.
2. Open the verification email sent by OnePass.
3. Click the **Verify Email** button or link.

### Verification Completion

- After clicking the link, you will be redirected to OnePass, and your email will be marked as **verified**.
