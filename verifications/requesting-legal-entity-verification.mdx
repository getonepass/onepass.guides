# How to Get a Legal Identity Verifiable ID / Request Verification

A **Legal Identity Verifiable ID** is a digital credential that confirms the legal existence of your company. This credential is required to verify your company’s identity on OnePass and is issued by our trusted verification partners.

## Why Do You Need a Legal Identity Verifiable ID?

- **Essential for verification** on OnePass.
- **Increases trust and credibility** with investors and partners.
- **Prevents fraud and ensures only legitimate companies are recognized.**

## How to Request a Legal Identity Verifiable ID

If your company does not yet have a **Legal Identity Verifiable ID**, follow these steps to obtain one from our verification partners:

<Steps>
### Determine Your Verification Partner

Depending on your company’s location, you need to request verification from the appropriate partner:

- **Bulgarian Companies** → Verification is handled by **BIA (Bulgarian Industry Association)**.
- **Companies from Other Countries** → Verification is provided by **FundingBox**.

### Submit a Verification Request

#### If Your Company is Based in Bulgaria (BIA):

1. Visit the **BIA Verification Portal** (insert link here).
2. Fill out the **Legal Identity Verification Form**.
3. Upload the required company documents (e.g., business registration certificate, tax ID, proof of address).
4. Submit your request and wait for approval.
5. Once approved, BIA will issue a **Legal Identity Verifiable ID** credential.

#### If Your Company is Outside Bulgaria (FundingBox):

1. Visit the **FundingBox Verification Portal** (insert link here).
2. Complete the **KYB (Know Your Business) Verification Form**.
3. Provide the necessary documents (e.g., business registration, incorporation certificate, tax ID).
4. Submit your request for review.
5. Once verified, FundingBox will issue your **Legal Identity Verifiable ID**.

### Receive and Store Your Credential
Once your request is approved, you will receive a **Legal Identity Verifiable ID**. This credential can be stored in a **holder wallet** that supports verifiable credentials.

### Use Your Credential to Verify on OnePass
After receiving your **Legal Identity Verifiable ID**, you can proceed with [verifying your company’s legal identity on OnePass](link to verification guide).
</Steps>
