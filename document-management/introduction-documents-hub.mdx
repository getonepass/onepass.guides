# Introduction to Documents Hub

Documents Hub is a secure and efficient feature within OnePass designed to help startups and investors manage, share, and collaborate on important documents. Whether you’re a startup preparing for fundraising or an investor conducting due diligence, Documents Hub provides the tools you need to streamline document workflows while maintaining security and privacy.  

---

## Key Features of Documents Hub

### 1. Secure Document Storage

- Store all your important documents in one centralized, secure location.  
- Protect sensitive information with OnePass’s decentralized identity framework.  

### 2. Document Sharing

- Share documents with investors, partners, or team members securely.  
- Create **public or password-protected sharing links** for controlled access.  

### 3. Version Control

- Track changes and updates with **version history**.  
- Restore previous versions if needed.  

### 4. Online Preview & Editing

- Preview almost any type of document directly in your browser.  
- Edit documents online without downloading them.  

### 5. Document Collaboration

- Collaborate with team members or investors in real-time.  
- Leave comments and suggestions directly on documents.  

### 6. Document Analytics

- Track how your documents are being accessed and used.  
- Gain insights into investor engagement and interest.  

### 7. Access Requests

- Investors can request access to specific documents from your public profile.  
- Approve or reject requests and grant **view or write access** as needed.  

---

## Benefits for Startups

### 1. Streamlined Fundraising

- Share key documents (e.g., pitch deck, financial statements) with potential investors effortlessly.  
- Reduce the time spent on manual document sharing and follow-ups.  

### 2. Enhanced Security

- Protect sensitive information with encrypted storage and sharing.  
- Control who can access your documents and how they can use them.  

### 3. Improved Organization

- Keep all your documents organized in one place.  
- Use folders and tags to categorize documents for easy access.  

### 4. Better Investor Engagement

- Provide investors with the information they need to make informed decisions.  
- Use document analytics to track investor interest and engagement.  

---

## Benefits for Investors

### 1. Efficient Due Diligence

- Access verified documents directly from startups’ profiles.  
- Request additional documents as needed with a single click.  

### 2. Secure Document Access

- View and download documents securely without worrying about data breaches.  
- Use password-protected links for added security.  

### 3. Collaboration Tools

- Collaborate with startups on shared documents in real-time.  
- Leave feedback and suggestions directly on documents.  

### 4. Document Analytics

- Track how startups’ documents are being used and shared.  
- Gain insights into the startup’s transparency and organization.  

---

## How Documents Hub Works

### For Startups

1. **Upload Documents:** Add key documents (e.g., pitch deck, financial statements) to Documents Hub.  
2. **Organize Documents:** Use folders and tags to keep documents organized.  
3. **Share Documents:** Create sharing links or approve access requests from investors.  
4. **Track Engagement:** Use document analytics to monitor how your documents are being accessed.  

### For Investors

1. **Request Access:** Request access to specific documents from a startup’s profile.  
2. **View & Download:** Access shared documents securely and download them if needed.  
3. **Collaborate:** Leave comments and suggestions directly on documents.  
4. **Track Usage:** Use document analytics to track how startups’ documents are being used.
